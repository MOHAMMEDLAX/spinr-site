Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 11, 2026  ·  Spinr App

Welcome to Spinr. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your data when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

Spinr is designed with a privacy-first approach. We collect minimal data:

  • Local Data: Topics, sessions, streaks, and achievements are stored locally on your device. We never access or transmit this data.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Notifications: If you grant permission, we send local push notifications for break reminders. No data is sent to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve core app features
  • To display advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To send local break reminder notifications (only with your permission)

3. Data Storage

All personal data is stored locally on your device using SQLite and AsyncStorage. We have no access to this data and it is never transmitted externally. Uninstalling the app deletes all your data.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: Used for in-app advertising. View Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Children's Privacy

Spinr is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us.

6. Your Rights

Since all data lives on your device, you have full control. You can delete all app data by uninstalling the application at any time. You may also disable notifications in your device settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
